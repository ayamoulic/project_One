My name is Aya Moulic

These are the few artists who inspire me:

─── ⋆⋅☆⋅⋆ ─── Elliot Smith ──── ⋆⋅☆⋅⋆ ────

Elliott Smith’s songwriting is quiet, introspective, and
honest, with soft melodies and poetic lyrics. His ability to
capture emotions in a simple yet meaningful way makes
his music inspiring. His sound feels personal and intimate,
which makes his songs easy to connect with. He is one of
Aya’s biggest inspirations, especially in how she applies
these characteristics to her own music.


───── ⋆⋅☆⋅⋆ ──── Alex G ──── ⋆⋅☆⋅⋆ ────

Alex G’s songwriting is experimental, intimate, and
unpredictable, blending lo-fi sounds with unique melodies
and cryptic lyrics. His ability to shift between genres while
maintaining a deeply personal feel makes his music
inspiring.His raw and unpolished style creates a sense of
honesty and nostalgia.


──── ⋆⋅☆⋅⋆ ─── Pacita Abad ─── ⋆⋅☆⋅⋆ ───

Pacita Abad was a Filipino artist known for her vibrant, textured paintings and trapunto techniques, as well as her advocacy for marginalized communities. Her work often highlighted social issues, including immigration, women’s rights, and global struggles, using bold colors and patterns to tell powerful stories. She is one of Aya’s biggest inspirations, especially in how she combined art with activism, creating meaningful and expressive work that speaks beyond aesthetics.
